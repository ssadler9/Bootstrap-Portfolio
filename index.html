About Me

My name is Sam Sadler. I am from Henderson, TX. There wasn't a lot to do in Henderson growing up, so I resorted to playing sports and video games! Golf has always been my passion. I have been playing for as long as I can remember. I'd have to say my favorite game was Halo 2!

After college, I got married to my best friend, Melanie. We lived in Houston for a while after we got married. I was the First Assistant Golf Professional at Woodforest Golf Club. We finally got to move back to Austin after I got a job at indeed! That career change made a huge impact on both of our lives. Needless to say, I wouldn't have gotten back into the world of Code without it!

I really enjoy spending time outside with my family. We have two dogs: Bo, he's a Labradoodle, and Aria, she's a minature schnauzer. We go on hikes, bike rides, camping trips etc.. I'm also a big gaming fan!
